Track 1: Life Hacks

From the Farmer‚Äôs Fridges in Main that provide convenient & healthy snacks, to using RockBot in Norris to choose the music, life hack projects should optimize different aspects of our lives to make them more easy, fun and/or accessible.

Track 2: Social Good

Projects in the social good category aim to make the world a better place through community-focused applications of technology. Social good projects can focus on (but are not limited to) topics such as accessibility, sustainability, and education.

Track 3: Health

Given how much COVID-19 has shaped the past few years, healthcare has become a pressing issue. Healthcare projects can focus on addressing public health challenges (ex: COVID-19 pandemic, food/drug safety, health literacy, etc) or personal health and wellness issues (ex: mental health, fitness, etc).
