WILDHACKS 2023

April 15-16, 2023

ABOUT.

WildHacks is Northwestern’s overnight Hackathon taking place in-person April 15-16 2023. As Northwestern’s largest hackathon, WildHacks is an opportunity for students to learn programming skills and develop a final project in a collaborative and inclusive environment. Through workshops, mentorship, and prizes, WildHacks aims to be an inclusive event that welcomes students of all skill levels, majors, and backgrounds.

Last year, we had 100 participants at WildHacks 2022! Check out the final projects here.


FAQ.

WildHacks is a weekend-long event where students work together to create cool software projects! You can expect helpful workshops, free food from Evanston restaurants, fun social & destress events, free swag, and more!

Don’t worry! WildHacks is open to students of all experience levels! We’ll send out resources with beginner friendly tutorials, and have a Discord channel where you can ask questions.

You can have teams of up to 4 people, and select teammates when you register or form them during the start of the event.

We’ll have events during the start of the Hackathon to form teams, and make sure you find a group to work with!

Attending WildHacks is 100% free, and we’ll provide meals and swag!
