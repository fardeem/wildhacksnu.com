TRACKS.

Track 1: Life Hacks

Blazing down Sheridan Road, electric scooters have allowed students to skip the long walks and zoom their way to class. Find the next shortcut that optimizes or makes an important aspect of our daily lives more accessible.

Track 2: Social Good

Social good is the concept of generating a positive impact for the public. The social good track is for hackers who strive to make the world more equitable using community-focused applications of technology. This can include creating for accessibility, sustainability, the community, etc.

Track 3: Healthcare

Given the impact of COVID-19 in the past 2 years, healthcare is more relevant than ever. Health-focused projects can take on public health challenges (ex: COVID-19, etc) or personal wellness (mental health, physical health, etc).
